Terms and Conditions

The Samskaara values your privacy and treats all financial details related to your transactions with the utmost confidentiality. Additionally, we do not share the email addresses or phone numbers of our donors or supporters with any third party.

Samskaara greatly appreciates all contributions that support our mission. To safeguard the privacy of our donors and their special relationship with us, we adhere to the following policies.

  • We may collect personal information online, including name, address, phone number, email address, and payment details, solely for the purpose of processing donations to Samskaara.
  • We do not share, sell, or trade donors' personal information with any third-party organizations.
  • Donors have the option to remain anonymous if they prefer not to be publicly recognized.
  • Donors can request at any time to opt out of receiving fundraising solicitations or other communications from us.
  • Donors may also choose to opt out of specific mailings, such as newsletters or event invitations.
  • We do not track, collect, or distribute personal information from visitors who browse our website.
  • All personal data collected through our website is encrypted using advanced security technologies to ensure maximum protection.
  • Our database and hosting servers adhere to industry-standard security measures, including secure logins, encryption, firewall protection, and intrusion detection systems to prevent unauthorized access.

  • To be informed about the organization's mission, how it intends to use donated resources, and its ability to effectively utilize donations for their intended purposes.
  • Donations will be used to support the organization’s relief and development programs. Any excess funds from a specific initiative will be redirected to other impactful programs as needed.
  • To be informed about the identity of the organization’s governing board members and to expect them to exercise responsible and ethical decision-making.
  • To have access to the organization’s most recent financial statements for transparency and accountability.
  • To receive appropriate acknowledgment and recognition for their contributions.
  • To be assured that information about their donations is handled with respect and confidentiality in accordance with applicable laws.
  • To expect professionalism in all interactions with individuals representing the organization.
  • To be informed whether those seeking donations are volunteers, employees, or hired representatives of the organization.
  • To have the option to remove their names from mailing lists that the organization may use for outreach purposes.
  • To be assured that their support is used in a responsible and ethical manner, aligned with the organization’s mission and goals.
  • To feel confident that the organization adheres to the highest standards of integrity and efficiency in handling donations.
  • To have the right to ask questions when making a donation and receive prompt, truthful, and transparent responses.

Policy on Donation Refunds

Samskaara follows a transparent and reliable refund policy to ensure our donors feel valued in their association with us. We take great care in processing donations as per the mandates provided by donors, both online and offline. However, in the unlikely event of an erroneous deduction or if a donor wishes to cancel their donation, we will address the request within 7 working days of receiving the complaint. The refund timeline will depend on the payment method used during the transaction. To process the refund, we require proof of the donation deduction and a written request from the donor within two days of the donation.

  • If a donation receipt has already been issued, the donor must return the original receipt to our official address to process the refund.
  • If a tax exemption certificate has already been issued, the donation refund will not be possible.
  • Refund requests will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis, and the final decision will be at the discretion of the organization.
  • All refunds will be processed via the original payment method used for the donation, and processing times may vary depending on the payment provider.
