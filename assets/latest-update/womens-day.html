Women’s Day

Every year on March 8th, we come together to celebrate International Women’s Day, a day dedicated to recognizing the achievements of women across all fields and advocating for gender equality. At Samskaara Foundation, we believe that empowering women through education, skill development, and community support is key to building a brighter future.

Our Mission

This year's campaign focuses on enhancing opportunities for women through career mentorship programs, financial literacy workshops, and leadership training. We are committed to ensuring that every women, regardless of the background, has access to the resources and support needed to achieve her dreams.

Key Highlights of the Campaign
  • Skill Development Workshops: Hands-on training sessions to help women develop practical skills for employment.
  • Educational Support: Scholarships and learning resources for women who wish to pursue further studies.
  • Women Entrepreneurs Program: Guidance and financial aid for women who want to start their own businesses
  • Health & Well-being Sessions:Awareness programs on physical and mental health, including self-care tips.
  • Community Awareness Drives: Public discussions and campaigns to highlight the importance of gender equality.

Why It Matters

Women play an essential role in shaping society, and it is crucial to provide them with equal opportunities. Empowered women empower communities. By supporting this campaign, you contribute to a more inclusive and progressive society where every women can thrive.

How You Can Participate

  • Attend our empowerment workshops and training sessions.
  • Share stories of inspiring women on social media using #SamskaaraWomen.
  • Volunteer to mentor or support women in your community.
  • Donate to fund education and skill programs for underprivileged women.

Join Us in Making a Difference!

We invite individuals, organizations, and businesses to collaborate with us in making this campaign a success. Together, we can create a world where women have equal opportunities to excel and lead.

Let’s make every day Women’s Day!
