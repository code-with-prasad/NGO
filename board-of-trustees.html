Board of Trustees

Home Board of Trustees

At Samskaara NGO, our Board of Trustees is the backbone of our organization, providing leadership, vision, and governance to ensure that we fulfill our mission of social welfare and sustainable development. The trustees bring a wealth of experience, dedication, and expertise from diverse fields, working collectively to uplift communities and create meaningful change.

We believe that strong leadership, integrity, and transparency are essential for an NGO to function effectively. Our trustees are responsible for setting strategic goals, overseeing the implementation of programs, and ensuring financial accountability. They guide our organization with a deep commitment to empowering underprivileged communities and fostering holistic development.

C.R. Ashwini
Akshatha gowda
