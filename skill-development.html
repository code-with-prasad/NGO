Skill Development

Home Skill Development

Receptionist Skills Training

Communication Skills – A receptionist must have clear verbal and written communication to greet visitors, answer calls, and handle inquiries professionally.

Customer Service – Providing a friendly and helpful experience for guests, handling complaints with patience, and ensuring customer satisfaction.

Organizational & Multitasking – Managing appointments, scheduling meetings, handling paperwork, and keeping the front desk area organized.

Technical & Computer Skills – Proficiency in using office software (MS Office, emails, databases) and handling phone systems for efficient day-to-day operations.

Professionalism & Etiquette – Maintaining a polite and professional attitude, following workplace etiquette, and dressing appropriately.

Time Management – Prioritizing tasks effectively to handle multiple responsibilities without delays.

Computer Skills Training

Basic Computer Literacy – Understanding how to use a computer, operating systems (Windows, macOS), and basic troubleshooting.

Typing & Data Entry – Improving typing speed and accuracy, using tools like MS Word and Excel for data entry.

Microsoft Office & Google Workspace – Learning MS Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Google Docs, Sheets, and Slides for office tasks.

Cybersecurity Awareness – Understanding online security, avoiding phishing scams, and creating strong passwords.

Basic Digital Communication – Using Zoom, Google Meet, and other collaboration tools for virtual meetings and teamwork.

Electro Tech Training

Fundamentals of Electrical and Electronics – Covers basic concepts like circuits, voltage, current, resistance, and electronic components.

Hands-on Practical Training – Focuses on real-world applications, including wiring, soldering, and troubleshooting electrical systems.

Safety and Compliance – Teaches important safety measures, electrical codes, and regulations to prevent hazards.

Use of Modern Tools & Equipment – Provides training on using oscilloscopes, multimeters, and other diagnostic tools in electrical engineering.

Industry-Relevant Skills – Prepares learners for careers in electrical engineering, automation, and electronics repair with practical knowledge.

Machinery Operation Training

Understanding Machine Components & Functions – Covers the parts, controls, and working mechanisms of various industrial machines.

Safety Protocols & Hazard Prevention – Emphasizes safety procedures, emergency handling, and compliance with industry regulations.

Hands-on Machine Handling – Provides practical experience in operating, maintaining, and troubleshooting machinery.

Efficiency & Performance Optimization – Teaches techniques to improve machine efficiency, reduce downtime, and enhance productivity.

Maintenance & Troubleshooting – Covers routine maintenance, diagnosing common issues, and ensuring machine longevity.

BPO & Telecaller Training

Communication & Soft Skills Development – Focuses on improving spoken English, active listening, and professional etiquette for effective customer interactions.

Call Handling Techniques – Teaches inbound and outbound calling strategies, script adherence, and dealing with difficult customers.

Sales & Customer Service Training – Covers persuasive communication, lead generation, and customer relationship management (CRM) techniques.

Technical & Process Knowledge – Provides an understanding of industry-specific processes, data entry, and using call center software.

Accent & Voice Modulation Training – Enhances clarity, tone, and pronunciation to improve communication with global clients.

Solar Technician Skills

Solar Panel Installation & Maintenance – Training on setting up, wiring, and maintaining photovoltaic (PV) systems for residential and commercial use.

Electrical & Wiring Knowledge – Understanding electrical circuits, connections, and safety measures related to solar power systems.

System Troubleshooting & Repairs – Identifying and fixing common issues in solar panels, inverters, and battery storage systems.

Energy Efficiency & Optimization – Learning to maximize solar power output and improve system performance.

Safety & Compliance Standards – Following industry regulations, environmental guidelines, and safety protocols for solar energy systems.
